A "winter wonderland" synonymous with alpine perfection, featuring the glacier-shaped Swiss Alps, crystal-clear lakes, and world-class luxury.

Best Time to Visit Japan

🌸 March – May (Spring): The best time to visit with cherry blossoms (sakura) and pleasant weather.

☀️ June – August (Summer): Warm season with festivals, but can be hot and humid.

🍁 September – November (Autumn): Beautiful fall colors with cool and comfortable weather.

❄️ December – February (Winter): Cold season, best for snow, skiing, and fewer crowds.

HOTELS IN JAPAN

  • 🏨 Park Hyatt Tokyo – A luxury hotel with stunning skyline views and elegant atmosphere.
  • 🏨 Shinjuku Granbell Hotel – A stylish and modern hotel in the heart of the city.
  • 🏨 Hotel Sunroute Plaza Shinjuku – A comfortable and affordable stay near major attractions.
    📍 Kyoto

    📍 Mount Fuji
  • 🏨 Kozantei Ubuya – A scenic lakeside hotel with breathtaking Mount Fuji views.
  • 🏨 Hotel Green Plaza Hakone – A relaxing hot spring hotel with Fuji views.
  • 🏨 K's House Fuji View Hostel – A budget-friendly stay with a direct view of Mount Fuji.

  • 🏨 Hotel Monterey Grasmere Osaka – A stylish hotel with great city access.
  • 🏨 Hotel Sunplaza – A simple and affordable stay for travelers.
    📍 Hiroshima
  • 🏨 Sheraton Grand Hiroshima Hotel – A modern luxury hotel near the train station.
  • 🏨 Hotel Granvia Hiroshima – A convenient and comfortable stay with easy access.

  • 🏨 Nara Hotel – A classic luxury hotel with traditional charm and history.
