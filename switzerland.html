VELARIS

A "winter wonderland" synonymous with alpine perfection, featuring the glacier-shaped Swiss Alps, crystal-clear lakes, and world-class luxury.

Best Time to Visit

🌸 Spring (April – June): Pleasant weather and blooming landscapes

☀️ Summer (July – September): Best for travel and outdoor activities

❄️ Winter (December – February): Snow, skiing, and winter wonderland

HOTELS IN SWITZERLAND

    📍 Zermatt

    Book here for a car-free, high-end alpine experience where almost every balcony offers a front-row seat to the iconic Matterhorn.

  • 🏨 Grand Hotel Zermatterhof – Luxury stay
  • 🏨 Mont Cervin Palace – Premium comfort
  • 🏨 Monte Rosa – Historic stay
    📍 Interlaken

    Choose this as your central basecamp if you want the best transport links to lakes and mountains, plus the widest variety of hostels and grand hotels.

  • 🏨 Victoria-Jungfrau – Luxury stay
  • 🏨 Beau Rivage – Premium comfort
  • 🏨 Hotel Interlaken – Budget stay
    📍 Lucerne

    Stay here to immerse yourself in medieval lakeside charm, with boutique hotels tucked into cobblestone streets just steps from the Chapel Bridge.

  • 🏨 Hotel Schweizerhof Luzern – Luxury stay
  • 🏨 Grand Hotel National – Premium comfort
  • 🏨 ibis Styles Luzern City – Budget stay
    Jungfraujoch

    While you can't stay on the peak, book a hotel in nearby Wengen or Grindelwald for early-morning access to the "Top of Europe" before the crowds arrive.

  • 📍 Kleine Scheidegg
    🏨 Hotel Bellevue des Alpes – Scenic mountain stay
  • 📍 Wengen
    🏨 Hotel Edelweiss Wengen – Budget + mountain view
  • 📍 Grindelwald
    🏨 Sunstar Hotel Grindelwald – Comfortable stay
    📍 Zurich

    Look for hotels in the Altstadt (Old Town) to blend luxury lakeside living with easy access to world-class dining and the main train station.

  • 🏨 Baur au Lac – Luxury stay
  • 🏨 Hotel Schweizerhof Zurich – Premium comfort
  • 🏨 ibis Zurich City West – Budget stay
    📍 Lauterbrunnen

    Book a room in this valley to wake up to the sound of waterfalls crashing down limestone cliffs right outside your window

  • 🏨 Hotel Staubbach – Scenic stay
  • 🏨 Hotel Silberhorn – Premium comfort
  • 🏨 Valley Hostel – Budget stay
