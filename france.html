VELARIS

France is a land of timeless elegance, known for its romantic cities, world-class art, iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, and exquisite cuisine.

Best Time to Visit France

🌸 April – June (Spring): The best time with pleasant weather, blooming flowers, and perfect sightseeing in Paris, Loire Valley, and Riviera.

☀️ July – August (Summer): Great for beaches in the French Riviera and outdoor festivals, but crowded and expensive.

🍁 September – October (Autumn): Fewer tourists, mild weather, and beautiful landscapes — ideal for relaxed travel.

❄️ November – March (Winter): Cold season, best for budget travel and enjoying Christmas markets and snow in some regions.
